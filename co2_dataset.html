CO2 Emissions Dataset

Our World in Data

Dataset Overview

A dataset containing information about CO2 emissions, GDP (Gross Domestic Product - the total value of all goods and services produced by a country), population, and other environmental metrics for countries around the world from 1750 to the present.

Global Coverage
Data from 195+ countries and regions worldwide
Historical Depth
270+ years of data (1750-2023)
Rich Metrics
CO2, GDP, population, per capita emissions
